‘La indecisión es la llave a la flexibilidad’

 

MañanaMañana.com is an archive of trips reports by a bunch of Irish whitewater kayakers, who operate under the motto 'Indecision is the Key to Flexibility.'

We are trying to push the boundaries of our sport; push them back to where they were about 10 years ago.
 
From September 2004 until July 2005, we went to the Americas.
In January 2007 we went on a two-week trip to Portugal.
In May 2007 we went to Italy.
