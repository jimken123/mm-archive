Sermenza

On the last day, the group headed off to run the Sermenza, which was a nice little wind-down run with a few interesting drops along the way. Does that sounds really vague? Well, truth be told, I can't really remember much about it other than there were two sections and some people got off after the top section, having burned out their love of kayaking. This is a temporary affliction that hits most kayakers from time to time. Sometimes getting off early for beer and ice cream can salvage a poor day.
